Project 1: Will a customer accept the coupon?

This project involves analyzing a dataset of customers who received a coupon and determining whether they accepted or rejected it. The idea is to identify patterns and trends that can help businesses understand customer behavior and improve their marketing strategies. The approach involves the following steps: - Data selection, cleaning and preprocessing - Exploratory data using python pandas - Create statistical summaries on what profile of users and conditions under which they accepted the coupon - Data visualization using matplotlib and seaborn - Results interpretation
